# What Are Your Strengths and Weaknesses?

## Why This Question Is Asked
To understand:
- Your self-awareness
- Your honesty
- How you handle improvement

---

## How to Answer Strengths

### Sample Strengths (Choose 1â€“2)
- Strong willingness to learn
- Good problem-solving approach
- Consistency and discipline
- Ability to work in a team

### Sample Answer
> "One of my strengths is my willingness to learn and improve.
> When I face something new, I take time to understand it deeply
> and practice until I am confident."

---

## How to Answer Weaknesses

### Rule:
- Never say something critical
- Always show improvement

### Sample Weakness
> "Earlier, I used to hesitate while asking questions,
> but I realized it slowed my learning.
> Now I actively ask doubts and clarify concepts."

---

## Common Follow-ups
- How are you improving yourself?
- Can you give a real example?
